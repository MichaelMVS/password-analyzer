What Makes a Strong Password?

Length

At least 12 characters is ideal. Longer passwords are harder to crack.

Variety

Mix uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and special characters.

Avoid Patterns

Don't use common words, sequences, or keyboard patterns.

Randomness

Use random combinations, not personal info or common words.
